about us

Explore CitiRide

CitiRide is the leading scooter sharing operator in Africa with over 10,000 electric scooters in 5 countries. CitiRide was born due to the continuous need of having a safe, reliable and efficient means of city commuting.

At its launch in Nairobi, CitiRide quickly realised scooter sharing perfectly met the demand of people looking for a convenient intra-urban mobility option.
