About Us

People want to zip through the city. They want to get from A to B quickly, easily, and conveniently. CitiRide through our e-scooters have been able to exactly provide this and revolutionise city transportation. The only thing you need is to subscibe and enjoy a very efficient, convenient and safe ride.

CitiRide is the leading scooter sharing operator in Africa with over 10,000 electric scooters in 5 countries. CitiRide was born due to the continuous need of having a safe, reliable and efficient means of city commuting.

Since its launch in Nairobi, CitiRide quickly realized scooter sharing perfectly meet the demands of people looking for a convenient intra-urban mobility and we have gone ahead and provided that need to our clients.
